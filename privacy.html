Privacy Policy

AmbientLoopSLite respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or share personal data directly. All core functionality, including mixing and playing ambient sounds, happens entirely on your device.

However, the app displays ads via AdMob. These ads may collect certain device information, including your advertising ID, to provide personalized ads. This data is handled by the ad provider, not by AmbientLoopSLite.

No other third-party analytics or trackers are used.
